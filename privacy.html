1. Overview

Welcome to Vedica Labs ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate the Vedica Labs website, mobile applications including HireIQ, Veer Gym .

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Services. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our Services.

🔒 TL;DR: We collect only what's necessary, never sell your data, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account registration data (name, email address, phone number)
  • Profile information (photo, bio, professional details)
  • Resume/CV uploads for HireIQ users
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay/Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Support requests and feedback submissions
  • Communications with our team

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device identifiers (device type, OS version, app version)
  • Usage data (features used, session duration, click events)
  • IP address and approximate location (city/country level)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Referral source (how you found us)

Information From Third Parties:

  • Social login data if you sign in via Google or LinkedIn
  • Analytics data from Firebase and Mixpanel

We use your data solely to provide and improve our Services:

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on AWS servers located in Mumbai, India (ap-south-1 region) with encrypted backups in Singapore.

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest for all user data
  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication for all internal systems
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • Strict role-based access control for employees
  • Automated vulnerability scanning and patch management

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide Services. You can request deletion at any time.

9. Family & Children's Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of families and children across the globe. This section outlines our comprehensive approach to child safety and family-friendly practices.

9.1 Our Commitment to Child Safety

  • Zero Tolerance for Harmful Content: We do not publish, host, or distribute any adult, explicit, or inappropriate content. All content on our platforms is family-friendly and age-appropriate.
  • Child-First Design: Our products designed for children are built with privacy-by-design principles, ensuring minimal data collection and maximum protection.
  • Verified Parental Consent: For children under the applicable age threshold, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information.
  • No Behavioral Advertising to Children: We never serve targeted advertisements to children or use their data for advertising purposes.

9.2 Global Children's Privacy Compliance

We comply with children's privacy laws from multiple jurisdictions:

  • COPPA (USA): Comply with the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. For children under 13, we collect only minimal information necessary for service delivery, with verifiable parental consent.
  • GDPR-K (EU): For children in the EU/EEA, services directed at children require parental consent at age 16 (or lower, down to 13, as defined by member states).
  • UK AADC (UK): Comply with the UK Age Appropriate Design Code, implementing high privacy defaults for children's data.
  • India DPDP Act: Comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 — processing children's data only with parental consent and for specific lawful purposes.
  • Australia: Comply with the Australian Privacy Principles and Online Safety guidelines for children's data.
  • Other Jurisdictions: We monitor and comply with emerging children's privacy regulations globally.

9.3 Types of Data We Collect from Children

When children use our services (with parental consent), we may collect:

  • Essential Data: Account credentials (email/username), parent-verified contact information
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and session data to improve user experience
  • Device Data: Device type and OS version for compatibility and security

We NEVER collect: Precise location data, photos, videos, or social connections from children without explicit parental consent.

9.4 Parental Rights & Controls

Parents and legal guardians have full control over their child's data:

  • Review: Request to view all data we hold about your child
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your child's account and all associated data
  • Revoke Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time
  • Access Control: Manage what data is collected through parental dashboard
  • Data Portability: Export your child's data in a portable format

9.5 Family Account Features

  • Family Link: Parents can link child accounts to their own for unified privacy management
  • Screen Time Controls: Parents can set usage limits and monitor activity (optional features)
  • Content Filters: Age-appropriate content filtering based on account settings
  • Activity Reports: Optional weekly summaries of child's app usage for parental oversight

9.6 Age Verification Processes

  • Self-Declaration: Users declare their age during account creation
  • Parental Verification: For ages below threshold, email/SMS verification of parent/guardian identity
  • Credit Card Verification: For higher age thresholds, optional credit card verification (no charge)
  • Government ID: As a last resort, government-issued ID verification for parental consent

9.7 Data Retention for Children's Data

  • Child data is retained only while the account is active
  • Upon parent request or account deletion, all child data is permanently erased within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for product improvement (cannot identify individual children)
  • Data backups are purged within 90 days of deletion

9.8 Reporting Concerns

If you suspect a child is being harmed or their privacy is being violated:

  • Immediate Report: Email safety@vedicalabs.in
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours for urgent matters
  • Law Enforcement: We cooperate fully with authorized law enforcement agencies
  • External Reporting: We work with organizations like NCMEC (US) and Internet Watch Foundation (UK)
🛡️ Our Promise: We will never monetize children's data. We do not sell, trade, or share children's personal information with third parties for any commercial purpose.
